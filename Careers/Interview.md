```text
My name is Farid Nubaili, and I am 24 years old. Currently, I work as a Fullstack Developer at Iotera Sinergi Digital. I have three years of experience in software development: one year in a full-time role and two years through freelancing and internships.

My most recent project was the Smart Gold Vault Inventory Management System for Bank Syariah Indonesia, which I developed under my current company. In this project, I mainly focused on the frontend using React and Ant Design Pro. My responsibilities included translating UI/UX designs into code, integrating APIs with the interface, and managing state interactivity.

In my previous company, I also worked as a Fullstack Developer, where I used Laravel for the backend and jQuery and AngularJS for the frontend. While I contributed to both sides, I primarily focused on backend development, which allowed me to gain solid experience in working with SQL and writing code for proper transaction handling.

I am passionate about continuously learning new technologies and documenting my learning process through GitHub README files. Recently, I have been exploring Golang and Flutter.

I am very interested in joining Nusantech Talentpool because it offers the opportunity to collaborate with professionals and global clients, which will enhance my expertise and provide valuable international exposure.

I believe this experience will help me grow further and open wider opportunities in the industry.
```
